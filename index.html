CSS

  • If you think of an elements as a box with contents
  • A margin indicated how much space we want around the outside of an element, the space between the outisde of the box and the rest of the world
  • A padding indicates how much space we want around the content inside the element, the space between the content of the box and the inside of the box that it is contained within
  • A border is the line that seperates the padding and the margin, it is the box we place our content in
  • We can define the thicknesses of each of those three wrappings; the padding, the box, the margin, through the CSS
